Event Silencer

Automatically silences your phone during Google Calendar events.

Event Silencer is a free Android app that automatically silences your phone during Google Calendar events. Stop worrying about forgetting to mute your phone before meetings, appointments, or important events — Event Silencer handles it for you.

How it works

When you sign in with your Google account, Event Silencer reads your calendar to detect when you have a busy event scheduled. It then automatically switches your phone to Silent, Vibrate, or Do Not Disturb mode (your choice) during those events, and restores your normal ringer when the event ends.

Features

  • Automatic silencing based on your Google Calendar
  • Three silence modes: Silent, Vibrate, or Do Not Disturb
  • Optional keyword filtering to silence only during specific events (e.g. "meeting", "appointment", "theater")
  • Silences 60 seconds before events start
  • Works in the background and survives phone reboots
  • Available in English, Hebrew, Arabic, Spanish, French, and Russian

Privacy

Event Silencer reads your calendar data locally on your device and never sends it to any server. No analytics, no tracking, no advertising.
